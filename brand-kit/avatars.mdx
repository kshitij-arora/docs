## Custom Avatars

Create AI avatars that represent your brand.

<Note>
  Custom avatars are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Creating a Custom Avatar

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Avatars**
2. Click **"+ Create a Custom Avatar"**
3. Upload reference images
4. AI generates your avatar

## External Avatar Integration

Connect third-party avatar services:

1. Click **"+ Connect your API Key"**
2. Enter API credentials
3. Access additional avatars
