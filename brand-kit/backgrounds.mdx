## Custom Backgrounds

Upload backgrounds that match your brand aesthetic.

<Note>
  Custom backgrounds are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Uploading Backgrounds

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Backgrounds**
2. Click **"Upload an Asset"**
3. Select your background image
4. Use in video editor

## Recommended Specs

- **Format:** PNG, JPG
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 recommended
