## Custom Glossary

Add brand names and key terms to ensure AI preserves their spelling in generated content.

<Note>
  Glossary is available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Adding Terms

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Glossary**
2. Type your term in the input field
3. Press **Enter** to add
4. AI will preserve exact spelling

## Use Cases

- Brand names (e.g., "Trupeer" not "True Peer")
- Product names
- Technical terminology
- Industry jargon
