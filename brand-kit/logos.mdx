## Custom Logos

Add your brand logo for use in videos and documents.

<Note>
  Custom logos are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Uploading Logos

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Logos**
2. Click **"Upload an Asset"**
3. Select your logo file
4. Use in videos and shared pages

## Recommended Specs

- **Format:** PNG (transparent background) or SVG
- **Size:** Min 200px width
