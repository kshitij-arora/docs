## Custom Music

Upload your own background music for personalized audio branding.

<Note>
  Custom music is available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Uploading Music

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Music**
2. Click **"+ Add New Music"**
3. Upload your audio file
4. Use in video editor's Music tab

## Supported Formats

- MP3
- WAV
- AAC

<Warning>
  Ensure you have licensing rights for any music you upload.
</Warning>
