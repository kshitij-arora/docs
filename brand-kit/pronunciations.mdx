## Custom Pronunciations

Define how specific words should be pronounced in AI-generated audio.

<Note>
  Pronunciations are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Adding Pronunciations

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Pronunciations**
2. Click **"+ Add New Pronunciation"**
3. Enter the word and phonetic spelling
4. AI uses custom pronunciation in videos

## Examples

| Word | Pronunciation |
|------|---------------|
| Trupeer | TROO-peer |
| API | A-P-I |
| SaaS | sass |
