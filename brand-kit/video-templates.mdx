## Video Templates

Save time by creating reusable video templates with your preferred settings.

<Note>
  Video templates are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Creating a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Go to **Brand Kit → Video Templates**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click **"+ Create a Template"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set your preferred video settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Name and save your template
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Template Settings

Templates can include:

- Default background
- AI voice selection
- Music settings
- Intro/Outro scenes
- Visual styles (shadows, padding, corners)
- Avatar settings

## Using Templates

When creating a new video:

1. Start a new recording
2. Select template from dropdown
3. All settings apply automatically

<Tip>
  Create templates for different content types - tutorials, demos, announcements.
</Tip>
