## Custom Voices

Create personalized AI voices for your videos.

<Note>
  Custom voices are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

## Your Custom Voices

View and manage your created voices with:

- Preview playback button
- Voice name and details
- Delete option

## Creating a Custom Voice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Go to **Brand Kit → Voices**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click **"+ Create a Custom Voice"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Samples">
    Provide voice recordings for AI training
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    AI creates your custom voice
  </Step>
</Steps>

## External Voice Integration

Connect third-party voice services:

1. Click **"+ Connect your API Key"** under "Connect External Voices"
2. Enter your API credentials
3. Access additional voice options
