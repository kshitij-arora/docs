## Overview

Trupeer is an AI video-creation platform. Record or upload a screen capture, and Trupeer generates a polished video with AI voiceover, avatars, zoom effects, transitions, and captions — plus an auto-generated how-to document — from a single source clip.

## The end-to-end workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a recording or upload a video">
    On [Home](/guides/home), click **Start Recording** to launch the Chrome extension, or **Upload a Video** to bring in an existing capture. See [Recording](/guides/recording) and [Upload](/guides/upload).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose language, template and output">
    Pick a recording language, apply a [video template](/guides/video-templates), and choose whether Trupeer should produce a video, a document, or both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let AI generate a draft">
    Trupeer transcribes the recording, writes a script, chunks it into sections, and renders an initial video and document. Minutes later you land in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine in the editor">
    Edit the [script](/guides/script-editor), add [visuals](/guides/visuals), [annotations and blur](/guides/annotations), [AI voices](/guides/voices), or an [AI avatar](/guides/avatars). Edit the [document](/guides/documents) in parallel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and share">
    Export, [publish to a Shared Page](/guides/shared-pages) with view/CTA controls, or add to your [Knowledge Base](/guides/knowledge-base).
  </Step>
</Steps>
