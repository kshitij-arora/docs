## Overview

Knowledge Base allows you to create beautiful, on-brand documentation sites for your videos and guides - perfect for internal training or customer-facing help centers.

<Warning>
  Knowledge Base is available as an **add-on** at $150/month.
</Warning>

## Creating a Knowledge Base

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Go to **Knowledge Base** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click **"+ Create Knowledge Base"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set up name, branding, and structure
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content">
    Add your videos and documents to the knowledge base
  </Step>
</Steps>
