## Team Management

The Organization feature allows you to create workspaces for team collaboration.

## Creating an Organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Go to **Organization** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Name">
    Enter your organization name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click **"Create Organization"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Inviting Team Members

1. Click **"Invite team"** from the top bar or user menu
2. Enter email addresses
3. Select role (Editor/Viewer)
4. Send invitations

<Note>
  Team members will receive an email invitation to join your organization.
</Note>

## Pending Invitations

View and manage pending invitations from the sidebar under **"X pending invitations"**.
