## What are Shared Pages?

Shared Pages allow you to share your videos and documents externally while tracking viewer engagement and analytics.

## Creating a Shared Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Content">
    Go to any video or document in your Library
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share">
    Click the **"Share"** button in the top right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set visibility, branding, and access options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Link">
    Copy the generated shareable URL
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

### Analytics Tracking

Monitor engagement for each shared page:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View Count" icon="eye">
    Total number of page visits
  </Card>
  <Card title="Watch Time" icon="clock">
    Average and total watch duration
  </Card>
  <Card title="Completion Rate" icon="check-circle">
    Percentage of viewers who finish
  </Card>
  <Card title="Geographic Data" icon="globe">
    Viewer locations (Enterprise)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Branding Options

<Note>
  Branded share pages are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

Customize your shared pages with:

- Custom logo
- Brand colors
- Custom CTAs (Call-to-Action buttons)
- Footer branding

### Access Control

Control who can access your content:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Public">
    Anyone with the link can view
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Password Protected">
    Viewers must enter a password
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Required">
    Collect viewer emails before access
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Shared Pages

Access all your shared pages from the **"Shared Pages"** section in the sidebar.

View and manage:

- Active shared links
- Analytics per page
- Sharing settings
- Revoke access

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Descriptive Titles">

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add CTAs">
    Include relevant call-to-action buttons to drive engagement (Scale+ plans).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor Analytics">
    Regularly check analytics to understand what content resonates with your audience.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
