## AI-Powered Translation

Trupeer makes it easy to translate your content into any language with a single click.

## Translation Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Record & Translate">
    **Record your screen** and translate instantly in one seamless flow.

    1. Click **"Translate Content"** on the dashboard
    2. Select **"Record your screen"**
    3. Choose your source language
    4. Choose target language(s)
    5. Record as usual - AI handles translation

    <Tip>
      Perfect for creating multi-language content from a single recording session.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Translate Existing">
    **Translate existing recordings** into any language with AI.

    1. Click **"Translate Content"** on the dashboard
    2. Select **"Upload an existing recording"**
    3. Upload your video
    4. Choose target language(s)
    5. AI translates script and regenerates voiceover
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
