## Video Creation Workflow

Trupeer makes it easy to create professional product videos in minutes. Here's the complete workflow.

### Step 1: Start Recording

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New Recording">
    Click **"Start Recording"** on the dashboard or **"+ Create new"** → **"Start recording"**.

    You'll see the **"Speak While Recording"** prompt:

    <Info>
      **Pro Tip:** Explain what you're doing as if you're talking to a friend. Your audio will help generate the AI script. No need to be perfect - AI will handle the rest!
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Upload Existing">
    Click **"Upload a Video"** to upload an existing recording for AI processing.

    Supported formats include MP4, MOV, and WebM.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 2: Record Your Screen

1. Select the screen, window, or browser tab to record
2. Ensure your microphone is enabled
3. Click to start recording
4. Narrate your actions while demonstrating
5. Stop when finished

<Warning>
  **Recording Limits by Plan:**
  - Free: 8 minutes max
  - Pro: 12 minutes max
  - Scale: 15 minutes max
  - Enterprise: Custom
</Warning>

### Step 3: AI Processing

After recording, Trupeer's AI will:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Transcribe Audio">
    Convert your voice narration to text
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Script">
    Create a polished script from your narration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify Key Moments">
    Detect important actions for zoom effects
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Timeline">
    Organize content into logical segments
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 5: Export & Share

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Export">
    Click the **Export** button to download your video as an MP4 file.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Share">
    Click **Share** to generate a shareable link. You can track:
    - View count
    - Watch time
    - Viewer engagement
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Speak Naturally">
    Don't read from a script - talk naturally as if explaining to a colleague. The AI will polish your narration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Show, Don't Tell">
    Demonstrate actions on screen while explaining. The AI will sync visuals with your narration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep It Focused">
    Cover one topic or workflow per video. Shorter, focused videos perform better.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Consistent Pacing">
    Maintain a steady pace - not too fast, not too slow. This helps AI generate better scripts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
