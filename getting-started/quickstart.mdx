## Create Your First Video

Follow these steps to create your first AI-powered product video.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [app.trupeer.ai](https://app.trupeer.ai) and create your account. You'll get a **10-day free trial** to explore all features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Recording">
    Click the **"Start Recording"** button on your dashboard, or use the **"+ Create new"** button in the top right.

    <Tip>
      When the recording prompt appears, remember: **Speak while recording!** Explain what you're doing as if talking to a friend. Your audio will generate the AI script.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record Your Screen">
    Select the screen or window you want to record. Narrate your actions naturally - no need to be perfect, the AI will handle the rest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit & Enhance">
    After recording, use the video editor to:
    - Edit the AI-generated script
    - Choose an AI voice
    - Add background music
    - Apply zoom effects
    - Add AI avatars
    - Customize visuals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export & Share">
    Click **Export** to download your video, or **Share** to create a shareable link with viewer analytics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Quick Actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Start Recording** | Begin a new screen recording with voice narration |
| **Upload a Video** | Upload an existing video for AI processing |
| **Create a Video** | Access video creation workflow |
| **Create a Document** | Generate step-by-step documentation |
| **Translate Content** | Translate existing content to new languages |

### Navigation Sidebar

- **Home** - Main dashboard with quick actions
- **Library** - All your videos and documents
- **Shared Pages** - Content shared externally
- **Brand Kit** - Branding customization
- **Knowledge Base** - Documentation libraries
- **Organization** - Team management
