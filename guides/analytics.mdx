## Overview

Analytics is the exploratory data surface of Trupeer. It exposes every measured event in your workspace as a queryable, visualizable data point — intended for ad-hoc investigation rather than fixed reporting.

## Query Model

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Event Stream" icon="wave-square">

  <Card title="Dimensions" icon="cube">

## Visualizations

Analytics supports line, bar, stacked, area, pie, and cohort visualizations, plus tabular breakdowns. Any chart can be saved as a widget for inclusion in a [Report](/guides/reports).

## Data Retention

Event-level data is retained per workspace policy (typically 13 months). Aggregated measures are retained indefinitely.
