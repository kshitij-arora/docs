## Overview

Annotations live in the **Elements** tab of the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor). Every overlay has a position on the canvas and a window on the timeline — drag in either to control where it appears and for how long.

## Text annotations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Elements → Add Text">
    A text block appears on the canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag to position">
    Move anywhere on the frame.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and style">
    Click to edit. Adjust color, font family, size, bold, italic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Time the appearance">
    Drag the block's timeline handles so it appears at the right moment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove">
    Select and **Delete**, or Backspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Shapes and blur

Rectangles, circles, and blur all live under **Elements**.

- **Shapes** — outline or solid, adjustable stroke width and color. Useful for highlighting UI regions.
- **Blur** — obscures everything underneath. Drag it over sensitive data (emails, account numbers, keys). Extend the blur across the full timeline if the data stays on screen.

Full-screen preview is recommended when positioning — small details are easier to align.

## Images

Insert images via **Elements → Image**. Either pick from your [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit) Logos library or **Upload**.

- Drag to position anywhere on the slide.
- Drag the timeline handles to control when the image appears.
- Drag the corner handles on the canvas to resize.

## Best practices

- **Blur sensitive data early** — the first pass of editing, before you tune visuals.
- **Avoid too many overlays** — one or two at a time keeps attention focused.
- **Prefer shapes over arrows** for persistent highlights; they read better at small player sizes.
