## Overview

An AI avatar adds a video presenter to the corner (or anywhere) of your capture. Trupeer ships a library of avatars and also integrates with **HeyGen** so you can bring your own likeness or an enterprise-licensed avatar.

## Enable an avatar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Avatar">
    In the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor), choose **AI Avatar** → toggle **Enable AI Avatar**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a style">
    Browse the Trupeer library — conversational, professional, or entertaining — and select one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize presentation">
    Set the avatar background (hex color), and pick a layout position — right bottom, top right, left bottom, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    The avatar re-renders into the video at the next preview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using your own HeyGen avatar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the avatar in HeyGen">
    In HeyGen, open **Avatar → Hyper-realistic Avatar**. Follow the template to record a ~30-second demo, upload the sample, and finish training.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your HeyGen API key">
    In HeyGen, **Profile → Account → Subscriptions and APIs** → copy the Agent API key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect HeyGen to Trupeer">
    In Trupeer, [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit) → **Connect your API key** → paste → **Connect Key**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use your avatar">
    Back in the Video Editor, **AI Avatar → Use Your Custom API Avatar Generation** → select your avatar → Submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Considerations

- **Credits** — avatar rendering consumes AI minutes per your plan. Check [Pricing](/guides/pricing) for your tier's allowance.
- **Voice pairing** — each avatar has a default voice, configurable under [Brand Kit → Avatars](/guides/brand-kit).
- **Placement** — corner positions are unobtrusive; full-slide avatars are best for title scenes.
