## Overview

The Calendar consolidates every time-bound artifact in your workspace — tasks with due dates, scheduled publications, meetings, and integrated external calendars — into a single temporal view.

## Views

Calendar supports day, week, month, and timeline views. The timeline view is particularly useful for editorial planning — it visualizes publication schedules across multiple content streams.

## Scheduling Workflows

- **Meeting creation** with automatic agenda document generation
- **Booking pages** — publish available slots for external parties to book, with optional [Contacts](/guides/contacts) record creation
- **Deadline extension** — shift task deadlines with cascading notification to assignees
