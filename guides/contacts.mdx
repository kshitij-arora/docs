## Overview

Contacts is Trupeer's lightweight CRM surface. It records external individuals and organizations with whom your workspace members interact — customers, prospects, partners, and vendors — and links them to related [Deals](/guides/deals), [Messaging](/guides/messaging), and [Tasks](/guides/tasks).

## Contact Model

<CardGroup cols={2}>

  <Card title="Company Records" icon="building">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Interaction Timeline" icon="timeline">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Fields" icon="pen-field">

  </Card>

## Data Input

Contacts can be created manually, imported from CSV, or synchronized from connected tools via [Integrations](/guides/integrations). Deduplication runs automatically against email addresses and company domains.

## Privacy and Scope

Contacts are workspace-scoped by default. Organization-tier customers can configure cross-workspace visibility under [Organization](/guides/organization) settings.
