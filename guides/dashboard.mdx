## Overview

The Dashboard is the primary analytical surface within Trupeer. It aggregates signals from across your workspace — usage, engagement, content production, and team performance — into a single, scrollable canvas.

<Frame caption="Dashboard — operational overview">
  ![Dashboard view](/images/0001_01_dashboard_initial.png)
</Frame>

## Layout

The dashboard is composed of four vertically-stacked regions, each addressing a distinct analytical concern.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Top summary">
    High-level KPIs and period-over-period deltas. Typically includes active users, content created, and engagement velocity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activity region">
    Real-time stream of workspace events — publications, comments, assignments, and integrations firing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content performance">
    Top-performing documents, most-viewed pages, and conversion-style metrics on shared content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Team productivity">
    Contributor-level breakdowns, workload distribution, and collaboration patterns.
  </Step>
</Steps>
