## Self-Serve Diagnostics

The Help surface exposes diagnostic tools useful before contacting support:

- **Workspace health check** — verifies integration connectivity and configuration
- **Export my data** — generates a portable archive of workspace content
- **Recent errors** — logs of client-side exceptions with anonymized context

## Escalation

For incidents affecting production operations, enterprise customers have access to a dedicated escalation channel configured under [Organization](/guides/organization) → Support.
