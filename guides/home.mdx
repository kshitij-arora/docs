## Overview

Home is the landing surface at [app.trupeer.ai](https://app.trupeer.ai). It exposes the two primary creation paths, a set of popular-feature entry points, and the most recent items you've worked on.

## Layout

- **Left sidebar**: Home, [Library](/guides/library), [Shared Pages](/guides/shared-pages), [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit), [Knowledge Base](/guides/knowledge-base), [Organization](/guides/organization).
- **Top bar**: Help, **Invite team**, **Create new**.
- **Main canvas**: Welcome block, creation CTAs, Popular features, Recent content grid.
- **Sidebar footer**: current plan, AI minutes remaining, Upgrade Plan, account menu.

## Recent content

The Recent content grid lists the most recently edited items across your Library and folders. Each tile shows the thumbnail, language, creator, and date. Click a tile to jump directly into the editor.

## What's new

The **What's new** button in the lower-left sidebar opens a popup of recently released features so you can try them from Home.
