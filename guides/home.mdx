## Overview

The Home screen is the default landing experience for every signed-in user. It surfaces the most relevant work across projects, teams, and content libraries, eliminating the need to search through nested menus to resume work.

<Frame caption="Home — initial view on sign-in">
  ![Home initial view](/images/0001_home_initial.png)
</Frame>

## Key Capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Recent Activity" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    A live feed of the documents, pages, and assets you last interacted with, sorted by recency.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Actions" icon="bolt">
    One-click access to frequent workflows such as creating a new document, inviting collaborators, or opening the Library.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personalized Feed" icon="sparkles">
    Content recommendations tailored to your role, team, and recent workspace patterns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workspace Shortcuts" icon="compass">
    Persistent entry points to pinned workspaces, favorite pages, and frequently-accessed tools.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
