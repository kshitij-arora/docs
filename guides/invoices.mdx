## Overview

Trupeer bills through LemonSqueezy (self-serve plans) and via contract for Enterprise. Invoices are retained for the lifetime of the workspace and are downloadable as PDF.

## Find your invoices

1. Open [Organization](/guides/organization) from the left menu.
2. Switch to the **Plan** or **Billing** tab.
3. Scroll to **Invoices**. Each row lists the date, amount, status, and a **Download** button.

## Update payment method

From the same Billing tab:

- **Update card** — re-authorizes the subscription against a new card.
- **Change billing email** — invoices are sent to this address on each renewal.
- **Change tax / company details** — reflected on subsequent invoices, not past ones.

## Enterprise billing

Enterprise customers receive invoices from the Trupeer finance team via email, following the agreed contract cadence. For invoice reissues or payment-detail changes, contact your account manager.

## Failed payments

A failed charge pauses generation but not viewing. You'll receive an email with a link to update payment; once settled, the plan resumes automatically.
