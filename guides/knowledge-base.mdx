## Overview

The Knowledge Base is a hosted help-center built from Trupeer-published Shared Pages. You assemble sections, drop in published videos and documents, and publish the whole thing as a branded site — optionally at your own domain — with AI search on top.

## Set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the Knowledge Base add-on">
    Knowledge Base is an add-on to paid plans. See [Pricing](/guides/pricing). Without it, the tab is still visible but publishing is gated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Knowledge Base → Manage">
    Left menu → **Knowledge Base**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create sections">
    Each section is a category (e.g. "Getting Started", "Admin", "Troubleshooting"). Name, reorder, and nest as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add content to sections">
    Drop in already-[Shared Pages](/guides/shared-pages) from your Library. Reorder within each section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Brand and publish">
    Set logo, colors, and theme; pick a subdomain (default `*.trupeer.ai/kb/...`) or configure a custom domain; publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reader experience

Visitors see:

- A sectioned left navigation with your articles and videos.
- Each entry is the Shared Page — video, transcript, and document.
- Global search across the whole site.

## AI search

With the **Knowledge Base with AI Search** add-on, the search box answers natural-language questions by retrieving relevant video moments and document passages.

- Answers cite the source page and, for videos, the exact timestamp.
- Search bundle includes an allotment of AI searches per month. See [Pricing](/guides/pricing) for the current cap.
- Works across every published page in the Knowledge Base.

## Custom domain

Available on Scale and above. Configure a CNAME to Trupeer's edge, add the domain in the Knowledge Base settings, and Trupeer provisions a TLS certificate. Existing `*.trupeer.ai` URLs continue to work as redirects.

## Branding

Every Knowledge Base respects your [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit):

- Logo top-left, linking to the KB home.
- Color palette applied to primary buttons and links.
- Typography applied to headings and body.
- Optional footer with copyright and links.

## Update published content

Editing a source video or document in Trupeer does not auto-update the KB. Re-publish the Shared Page, and the KB entry updates on the next build.
