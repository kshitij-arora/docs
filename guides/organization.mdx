## Overview

The Organization surface is where you administer your Trupeer workspace — invite teammates, assign roles, add or remove seats, and view usage analytics. It's the shared-state layer on top of everything else.

<Frame caption="Organization — members and settings">
  ![Organization view](/images/0006_organization.png)
</Frame>

## Members

The **Members** tab lists everyone on the workspace with their email, role, and status. From this table you can:

- **Add Member** — invite a new teammate by email; they receive a sign-up link.
- **Change role** — toggle a member between Admin and Editor (and Viewer where applicable).
- **Remove** — revoke access. Their created content stays in the workspace.

### Roles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="shield-halved">
    Full workspace control — billing, members, integrations, and all content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Editor" icon="pen">
    Can create, edit, and publish videos and documents. Cannot modify billing or members.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Viewer" icon="eye">
    Read-only access to shared content. Cannot author.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

See [Permissions & Roles](/reference/permissions) for the full capability matrix.

## Seats and billing

<Frame caption="Organization — seats and plan view">
  ![Organization scrolled](/images/0007_organization_scrolled.png)
</Frame>

- **Seats used / Seats available** — visible at the top of the Members tab.
- **Add Seats** — opens a purchase flow for additional seats on the current plan.
- **Plan** — current plan tier, renewal date, and upgrade/downgrade actions.
- **Invoices** — historical invoices (see [Invoices](/guides/invoices)).

## Analytics

The **Analytics** tab surfaces workspace-level usage:

- Videos created per member / per time window.
- Published Shared Pages and view counts.
- Minutes consumed against the plan's AI minute budget.
- Knowledge Base views and AI search usage (when the KB add-on is active).

## SSO and SCIM

Available on Enterprise. Configure SAML or OIDC identity providers and SCIM v2 provisioning from the **Security** tab. Contact sales for onboarding.

## Custom domain

Available on Scale and above. Attach a domain for Shared Pages and the Knowledge Base from the **Domain** tab.
