## Overview

Organizations are the highest-level container in the Trupeer data model. They are designed for enterprises that operate multiple workspaces under a single corporate entity — consolidating billing, SSO, and compliance controls.

## When to Use Organizations

- Multiple distinct business units or departments each running their own workspace
- Centralized procurement with decentralized product usage
- Regulatory requirements for uniform policy enforcement

For single-workspace customers, most equivalent controls are available at the [Settings](/guides/settings) level.
