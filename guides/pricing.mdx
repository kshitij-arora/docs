## Overview

Trupeer is offered across three commercial tiers. Plan selection determines seat capacity, feature availability, support tier, and compliance posture.

<Frame caption="Pricing — plan comparison">
  ![Pricing view](/images/0008_pricing.png)
</Frame>

## Plan Structure

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Starter" icon="seedling">
    Single-workspace usage for small teams. Core content surfaces, basic [Integrations](/guides/integrations), community support.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Professional" icon="rocket">
    Multi-workspace support, advanced analytics, priority support, full integration catalog.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="building-columns">
    Organization-level controls, SSO, SCIM provisioning, custom contracts, dedicated success management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom" icon="handshake">
    Negotiated terms for large deployments, on-premise variants, and specialized compliance requirements.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Billing Cadence

<Frame caption="Pricing — yearly billing">
  ![Pricing yearly](/images/0011_pricing_yearly.png)
</Frame>

Plans can be billed monthly or annually. Annual billing is typically discounted relative to month-to-month pricing; discount percentage is displayed at the point of plan selection.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Frame caption="Pricing — FAQ section">
  ![Pricing FAQ](/images/0010_pricing_faq_expanded.png)

The pricing page includes an expandable FAQ addressing seat counting, trial policy, plan transitions, and cancellation terms.

## Upgrading

<Frame caption="Pricing — plan upgrade modal">
  ![Upgrade plan modal](/images/0018_upgrade_plan_modal.png)
</Frame>

Plan upgrades are self-service for Starter and Professional tiers. Enterprise transitions typically involve a sales engagement for contract alignment.
