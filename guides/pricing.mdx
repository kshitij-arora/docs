## Billing cadence

Plans bill monthly by default. Annual billing is discounted; the percentage is shown at checkout.

## FAQ

**Do seats roll over?**
No. Unused seats don't carry month to month — the plan bills for the seat count on the renewal date.

**What counts as an AI minute?**
Any generation that produces speech — a new video, a re-rendered voice, or a translation. Script edits alone don't consume minutes.

**Can I cancel any time?**
Yes. Cancellation stops renewal at the end of the current period; your content stays accessible.

**What happens when my free trial ends?**
Content stays in view-only mode. New generations are blocked until you upgrade.

**Is there a student or non-profit discount?**
Contact sales — discounts are handled case by case.

## Upgrading

Plan upgrades are self-service from **Organization → Plan**. Enterprise transitions go through a sales conversation to align contract terms.
