## Overview

The Profile section is the canonical surface for managing your individual identity within a workspace — distinct from workspace-level or organization-level configuration.

<Frame caption="User profile page">
  ![Profile page](/images/0012_profile.png)
</Frame>

## What You Can Configure

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Identity" icon="id-card">
    Display name, avatar, pronouns, role, and the bio surfaced to collaborators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Authentication" icon="shield-halved">
    Password, connected single sign-on providers, active sessions, and multi-factor authentication.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications" icon="bell">
    Per-channel notification preferences — email, in-app, and integrations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personalization" icon="palette">
    Theme, accent color, default landing screen, and keyboard shortcut preferences.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Scope

Profile settings apply across every workspace you belong to. For workspace-specific configuration, see [Settings](/guides/settings) or [Organization](/guides/organization).
