## Overview

Trupeer records your screen through a Chrome/Edge extension, then generates a studio-quality video and a how-to document from the capture. Recording supports 20+ languages and pauses in natural moments so you don't have to reshoot.

## Prerequisites

- A supported browser — **Chrome** or **Edge**.
- The Trupeer extension installed from the Chrome Web Store.
- Microphone permission granted to the browser and to Trupeer.

## The recording flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from Home">
    On [Home](/guides/home), click **Start Recording**. If the extension isn't installed, Trupeer prompts you to install it from the Chrome Web Store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your microphone">
    Pick the input you want Trupeer to transcribe. The floating toolbar lets you pause, cancel, toggle cursor highlight, and restart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the recording language">
    Pick from 20+ supported languages. The selected language drives transcription and the generated script.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant screen access">
    Choose **This Tab**, **A Window**, or **Entire Screen**. A countdown appears, then recording starts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record the session">
    Narrate what you're doing. Take pauses — Trupeer can trim silence and re-order sections later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stop and hand off to AI">
    Click **Stop**. Trupeer uploads the capture, transcribes it, and generates a draft video + document.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Microphone permissions

If Trupeer cannot hear your mic:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
    Accept the browser prompt, then ensure **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone** allows both Chrome and Trupeer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edge" icon="edge">
    Accept the **Speak While Recording** tips, then in **Windows Settings → Privacy & security → Microphone** enable Edge and desktop app access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Tips for a clean recording

- **Close distractions** — quit notifications and email clients before starting.
- **Narrate deliberately** — say what you're clicking and why. The AI script inherits your voice and structure.
- **Use short pauses** — they become natural scene breaks and are easy to trim later.
- **Don't worry about slips** — edit the transcript in the [Script Editor](/guides/script-editor); no reshoot needed.

## After stopping

Trupeer lands you on the draft screen. From there you can:

- Apply a [video template](/guides/video-templates) and a [document format](/guides/doc-templates)
- Toggle **Use Creator's Original Voice** (see [Voices](/guides/voices))
- Pick the output — video, document, or both

Then click **Generate AI Content** to produce the draft and open the editors.
