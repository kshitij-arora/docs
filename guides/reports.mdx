## Overview

Reports provides a structured layer above raw [Analytics](/guides/analytics) data. Where Analytics exposes interactive exploration, Reports captures fixed, reproducible views designed for recurring review and distribution.

## Report Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Operational" icon="gauge">
    Activity and throughput — content created, tasks completed, meetings held.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Engagement" icon="chart-line">
    Reader and collaborator behavior — views, edits, dwell time on [Shared Pages](/guides/shared-pages).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Revenue" icon="chart-pie">
    Pipeline and revenue analytics derived from [Deals](/guides/deals) records.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom" icon="chart-simple">
    Workspace-authored reports using the query builder over any data source.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Delivery

Reports can be viewed interactively, exported to CSV or PDF, or scheduled for automatic delivery. Scheduled reports are sent via email or posted to a connected [Messaging](/guides/messaging) channel.

## Access Control

Report visibility follows workspace permissions. Sensitive reports — revenue, contractual data — can be gated to specific roles or teams.
