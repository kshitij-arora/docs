## Overview

Trupeer turns your recording into a structured script. The Script Editor is a rich text surface where every chunk maps to a slice of the video — edit the words, and the video follows.

## Editing the script

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Script">
    In the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor) sidebar, open the **Script** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit text">
    Select any passage to edit, delete, or type new content. Click **Keep Changes** to commit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert new lines">
    Position the cursor and type. The script updates in place; the video re-renders the affected chunk on the next voiceover refresh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh Voiceover">
    Click **Refresh VoiceOver** to regenerate audio for edited chunks only — unchanged chunks are preserved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trim and Untrim

Trim removes content by selection rather than by time.

- Highlight text → **Trim** in the inline popup → the selection disappears from the video.
- Select a whole paragraph → **Trim** → the paragraph is removed.
- Click trimmed (struck-through) text → **Untrim** → restores it.

Because trims are selection-based, they are non-destructive and fully reversible.

## Skip chunks

Skipping excludes a whole chunk from playback without deleting it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the chunk menu">
    Click the **Delete** action next to a chunk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the skip">
    The chunk moves to the **Skips** list. Video duration updates automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore a skip">
    Click **Unskip** under the chunk and confirm. Refresh the voiceover to re-render.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pauses

Pauses insert timed silence where the narration needs breathing room.

1. Select the text where you want a pause inserted.
2. Click **Pause** in the popup and pick a duration.
3. Save with **Keep Changes** and refresh the voiceover.

To remove a pause, hover the marker and press **Backspace**, then re-save.

## Original transcript

If you've edited the script extensively and want to start over, open **Use Original Transcript** — Trupeer reverts to the first AI-generated script. Re-applying is reversible via **Keep Changes**.

## Find and Replace

Accessed from the editor toolbar, Find and Replace supports case-sensitive matching, single replace, and **Replace All**. It also runs in the [Document Editor](/guides/documents).
