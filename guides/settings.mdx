## Overview

Settings exposes every workspace-level configuration surface. Changes made here apply to all members of the active workspace and are distinct from personal [Profile](/guides/profile) preferences.

## Access Control

Settings surfaces are permission-gated. Members typically see a read-only view of general settings, while administrators and owners can modify all configuration domains.
