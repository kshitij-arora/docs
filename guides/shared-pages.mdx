## Overview

Shared Pages is the publication layer of Trupeer. It takes internal content — a document, a Knowledge Base article, an entire folder — and exposes it at a shareable URL with configurable access controls.

## Access Modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Public Link" icon="link">
    Anyone with the URL can view. Ideal for marketing collateral and public documentation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Password-Protected" icon="key">
    URL plus a shared password — a middle ground for distribution without identity management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Allowlist" icon="envelope-circle-check">
    Readers verify via email. Every view is attributed and auditable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SSO-Gated" icon="shield-halved">
    Enterprise-tier — viewers must authenticate via a configured identity provider.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Page Management

<Frame caption="Shared Pages — listing and controls">
  ![Shared Pages scrolled](/images/0017_shared_pages_scrolled.png)
</Frame>

Each published page exposes the following controls:

- **Expiration** — automatic un-publication at a scheduled time
- **View analytics** — unique visitors, time-on-page, drop-off points
- **Custom domain** — serve the page under your own subdomain
- **Branding** — apply workspace [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit) styling
- **Version pinning** — freeze the published content against future internal edits

## Audit and Compliance

Every view, export, and access-mode change is logged. Audit data is exportable from the [Organization](/guides/organization) administration surface.
