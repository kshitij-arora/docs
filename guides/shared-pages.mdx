## Overview

A Shared Page is the public surface for a finished video + document pair. Publish from the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor) or [Library](/guides/library), and Trupeer serves the content at a shareable URL with embedded player, scrollable transcript, and the generated document alongside.

## Publish a video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the video">
    From the Library, open the video you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Publish / Share">
    Top right of the Video Editor. Confirms the current draft is what gets published.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick visibility">
    **Public** (anyone with the link) or **Unlisted** (link-only, not indexed). Both produce a `trupeer.ai/s/...` URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the link">
    Share it in chat, email, or embed into your own site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What readers see

A published page includes:

- The rendered video with play controls.
- A synced transcript on the side — click a line to jump to that moment.
- The [Document](/guides/documents) — summary, steps, inline annotated screenshots.
- Download / Export affordances if enabled.
- Your workspace branding from the [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit).

## Embed on your site

Each Shared Page has an **Embed** option that returns an `<iframe>` snippet. Paste it into a blog post, help center, or landing page. The embed inherits the page's branding and permissions.

## Manage published pages

<Frame caption="Shared Pages — listing and controls">
  ![Shared Pages scrolled](/images/0017_shared_pages_scrolled.png)
</Frame>

From the **Shared Pages** tab in the left menu, each card lets you:

- **Copy link** — share the public URL
- **Open** — view as a reader would
- **Unpublish** — revoke access; the URL 404s thereafter
- **Republish** — push the latest draft edits to the live page

Edits to the source video or document are **not** automatically live — they publish when you click **Republish**.

## Branding and custom domain

- Pages pick up your Brand Kit logo, colors, and fonts.
- Custom domain (e.g. `docs.yourcompany.com`) is available on the Scale plan and above. Configure from **Organization → Domain**.

## Analytics

Each page records view counts and unique visitors, visible on the Shared Page card. For deeper analytics (watch-through, drop-off), use the [Organization](/guides/organization) analytics tab.
