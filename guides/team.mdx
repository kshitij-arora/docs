## Overview

The Team section surfaces every member of the active workspace. It is the authoritative directory for discovering collaborators, reviewing activity, and managing role assignments.

## Capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>

    Searchable list of all active members with role, team affiliation, and last-active timestamp.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Role Assignment" icon="user-shield">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Groupings" icon="users">

  </Card>

  </Card>

## Permission Model

Permissions in Trupeer follow a least-privilege model. A member's effective permissions are the union of their workspace role and any team-level grants.

| Role | Typical Capabilities |
|------|---------------------|
| Owner | Full control; billing; delete workspace |
| Admin | Manage members, settings, integrations |
| Member | Create and edit content; invite guests |
| Guest | Scoped read or edit access to specific pages |
