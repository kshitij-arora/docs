## Overview

If you already have a raw recording, you can hand it directly to Trupeer. Upload supports desktop and mobile recordings, auto-detects the aspect ratio, and gives you trim, crop, and output controls before AI generation runs.

## The upload flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start an upload">
    On [Home](/guides/home) click **Upload a Video**, then **Select a Video** and choose a file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the recording language">
    The selected language drives transcription and the generated script.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a template">
    Choose a [video template](/guides/video-templates) — marketing demo, onboarding, sales demo, or a custom template you saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trim to save AI minutes">
    Drag the in/out handles to cut unused head and tail. Trimmed time does not count toward your plan's AI minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Crop to hide sensitive info">
    Use the crop tool to remove areas of the frame you don't want visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure output">
    Toggle **Video**, **Document**, or both. Optionally enable **Use Creator's Original Voice** to keep your narration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Trupeer processes the upload and opens the draft once ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mobile recordings

Vertical mobile captures are supported natively. Trupeer detects the aspect ratio automatically and can also convert to wider resolutions during editing. All post-generation tools — AI voice, background music, intro/outro slides, zoom, avatars, annotations, blur — work on mobile-sourced videos.

## Trim vs. crop

- **Trim** — removes time at the start and end of the clip. It's the single biggest lever for saving AI minutes.
- **Crop** — removes pixels from the frame; useful for hiding tabs, account details, or chat windows.

Both can be re-applied later in the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor).
