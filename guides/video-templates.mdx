## Overview

A video template bundles every stylistic decision into one named preset. Apply it before generation, or switch a finished video onto it, and every setting — voice, music, intro/outro, avatar, shadows, padding, zoom, captions, aspect ratio — updates together.

## Create a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Brand Kit → Video Templates">
    In the left menu, open [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit) → **Video Templates** → **Create a Template**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Voice">
    Pick a default voice from your Trupeer or custom voices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Music">
    Select a default music track and volume level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visuals">
    Set the background, intro/outro slides, shadow, rounded corners, padding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Avatar">
    Choose whether an avatar is enabled, its size, background color, and position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Other defaults">
    Zoom on/off, captions on/off, aspect ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Name the template and save. It appears under **Your Templates** in the Video Templates tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Apply a template

### During generation

On the **Generate AI Content** screen (post-recording or post-upload), pick a template from the selector. Trupeer's defaults include Marketing, Onboarding, Sales Demo, and similar presets.

### After generation

From the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor) bottom bar, open the template selector and pick a different template. All covered settings update; script/trim/skip edits are preserved.

## Manage templates

In the **Video Templates** tab, each template card has a three-dot menu:

- **Rename**
- **Set as default** — applied to all new videos by default
- **Settings** — reopen the builder
- **Delete**

## When to use templates

- **Brand consistency** — one template per product line or customer-facing surface.
- **Speed** — avoid re-picking voice, music, avatar, and layout per video.
- **Team standardization** — a default template ensures new teammates produce on-brand output without setup.
