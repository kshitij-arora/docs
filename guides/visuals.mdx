## Overview

Everything in the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor) that shapes what the viewer *sees* — framing, zoom, transitions, backgrounds, slides, captions — is grouped here. Each of these works on top of the script and voiceover without requiring a re-render.

## Backgrounds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Visuals → Background">
    Toggle the background **On** to show a backdrop behind the capture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick or upload">
    Choose a pre-licensed background, or upload one via [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit) → Backgrounds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tune presentation">
    Add a drop shadow, round the corners (16 is subtle, 100 is fully circular), and set padding to shrink the inset video relative to the background.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Intro and outro slides

Intro/outro slides are editable on a Fabric.js canvas — add text, tweak font and size, upload a logo, and pick a background video.

- Click **Intro Scene** to edit text, fonts, logo, and duration. Click **Save Changes** to apply.
- Repeat for **Outro Slide**. Toggle **Disable Slide** to hide it; **Add Outro Slide** restores it.
- Transitions can be set per slide — pick a style and duration.

## Scenes

Scenes are full-screen inserts that break up the capture — useful for titles, section dividers, or agenda slides.

1. **Script → Add → Add Scenes**, then click the **+** in the Scenes section.
2. Upload or pick a background, position a logo, add a text box.
3. Configure entry/exit transitions (style, duration, direction).
4. Optional — enable **AI Voiceover** for the scene and write prompt text.
5. **Save Changes**, preview, or delete.

## Chapters

Chapters segment a long video into navigable sections.

- **Script → Add → Add Chapters**.
- **Generate with AI** for automatic chapter breaks, or **Add Manual** to author them.
- Rename chapters inline; use the three-dot menu for per-chapter options.
- Chapters appear on Shared Pages as a navigable sidebar.

## Zoom

Zoom effects magnify a region of the capture for focus.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Per-chunk zoom" icon="magnifying-glass-plus">
    In the **Zoom** tab, pick a zoom position and resolution. Set speed with the timeline handles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timeline zoom" icon="magnifying-glass">
    The eye icon below the preview toggles a secondary timeline you can magnify for precise scrubbing.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Delete a zoom via **Delete Zoom** in the zoom detail panel.

## Transitions

Transitions animate cuts between clips, between scenes, and on intro/outro.

1. Hover a clip, scene, or slide → **Add Transitions**.
2. Pick an entry style, an exit style, and a duration.
3. **Save** and preview. Repeat per boundary as needed.

## Aspect ratio

Under the timeline, **Modify Aspect Ratio** offers:

- **Auto** — detect from the source
- **Wide** — 16:9
- **Vertical** — 9:16

The preview updates instantly.

## Trim and crop the video

- **Trim Video** on the timeline — drop the scissor button, drag to select, confirm to cut.
- **Crop Video** — drag crop handles, then **Save Changes**.

Both are non-destructive until export.

## Captions

Above the preview, **Turn On Captions** adds auto-generated captions that appear in preview and in the exported video.
