## Overview

Every Trupeer video has exactly one audio track generated from the [script](/guides/script-editor). You choose the voice — a Trupeer preset, your own original narration, or a voice you've created elsewhere and connected via API.

  <Card title="Trupeer AI voices" icon="microphone">

## Switch voices in the editor

1. In the [Video Editor](/guides/video-editor), open the **AI voice** panel.
2. Pick a voice. The list includes Trupeer presets, any custom voices you've saved, and external voices if connected.
3. Click **Refresh VoiceOver**. Only chunks that changed since the last render re-generate.

## Retain the original voice

### From the recorder

After stopping the recording, open **Volume settings** and enable **Retain your original voice**. Then click **Generate AI Content**.

### From an upload

On the upload screen, check **Use Creator's Original Voice** before **Submit**.

In both cases, the AI still transcribes and chunks your audio; edits in the script regenerate only affected segments.

## Create a custom voice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Voice → Create a new voice">
    In the Video Editor or in [Brand Kit → Voices](/guides/brand-kit).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the voice">
    Use a clear identifier — e.g., "Manik's Voice".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide a sample">
    Upload an audio file, or record directly in the browser. ~5 minutes is the recommended minimum for a good clone; record in a quiet environment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and use">
    The voice appears in the voice picker and in the Brand Kit Voices tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect ElevenLabs

1. In [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit), click **Connect your own API key**.
2. Pick **ElevenLabs** as the provider and paste your key.
3. Back in the Video Editor **AI voice** panel, enable **Custom API voice generation**.
4. Select a personal ElevenLabs voice and refresh the voiceover.
