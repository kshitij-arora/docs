## Overview

A Workspace is the primary organizational unit in Trupeer. Every page, document, asset, and member belongs to a workspace. Users can belong to multiple workspaces and switch between them from the sidebar footer.

## Common Operations

- **Create a workspace** — from the workspace switcher in the sidebar footer
- **Switch workspaces** — keyboard shortcut or menu-driven
- **Leave or archive** — self-service from [Settings](/guides/settings)
- **Transfer ownership** — administrator-gated operation under Settings → Members
