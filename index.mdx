## Introduction

<video src="/videos/WhatsApp-Video-2026-04-17-at-12.56.36.mp4" controls />

Trupeer turns a screen recording into a polished, voiced video plus a structured how-to document. This site documents every surface, workflow, and integration.

Documentation is split into two sections:

- **Guides** — task-oriented walkthroughs of each surface
- **Reference** — structured maps, permissions, shortcuts, and glossary
