## What is Trupeer?

Trupeer.ai is an **AI-powered platform** for creating professional product videos and documentation guides. Transform your screen recordings into polished, professional content with AI-generated scripts, voiceovers, and automatic documentation.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Video Creation">
    Create studio-quality product videos with realistic AI voiceovers, avatars, zoom effects, and more. Simply record your screen while explaining what you're doing - the AI handles the rest.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Automatic Documentation">
    Every video automatically generates a step-by-step guide with screenshots, timestamps, and formatted instructions. No manual documentation needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multi-Language Translation">
    Translate your content into any language with a single click. AI handles voiceover regeneration and text translation seamlessly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Extensive Brand Kit">
    Customize every aspect of your content - from custom AI voices and avatars to branded templates, backgrounds, and intros/outros.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Collaboration">
    Work together with shared workspaces, folder organization, and team management features.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
