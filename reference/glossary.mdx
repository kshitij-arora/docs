---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Canonical definitions for terms used throughout the Trupeer platform and documentation."
---

## A

**Admin**
A workspace role with elevated permissions — member management, settings configuration, and integration administration. Does not include billing ownership.

**Asset**
Any stored file or object within the [Library](/guides/library) — documents, images, video, and templates.

**Audit Log**
An immutable, append-only record of security-relevant events. Available to Admin and Owner roles.

## B

**Brand Kit**
The centralized store of visual and written brand guidelines. See [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit).

## C

**Canvas**
The main content area of any screen — contrasted with the sidebar, top bar, and contextual panels.

**Category**
A hierarchical grouping within the [Knowledge Base](/guides/knowledge-base). Categories can nest and inherit permissions.

**Collection**
A saved view of Library assets defined by filter criteria rather than folder membership.

## D

**Dashboard**
The primary analytical surface exposing operational KPIs. See [Dashboard](/guides/dashboard).

## G

**Guest**
A restricted role with scoped access to specific content. Guests cannot invite other users or access workspace settings.

## K

**Knowledge Base**
The article-oriented content surface for long-form, evergreen documentation. See [Knowledge Base](/guides/knowledge-base).

## L

**Library**
The centralized asset catalog. See [Library](/guides/library).

## M

**Member**
The default workspace role. Can create content and invite guests, but cannot modify settings or billing.

## O

**Organization**
The enterprise-tier container grouping multiple workspaces. See [Organization](/guides/organization).

**Owner**
The highest workspace role with billing ownership and the ability to delete the workspace.

## P

**Page**
A single, URL-addressable content unit. Can be published internally or externally via [Shared Pages](/guides/shared-pages).

## R

**Role**
A workspace-level permission tier. Trupeer supports four roles: Owner, Admin, Member, and Guest.

## S

**SCIM**
System for Cross-domain Identity Management. Used by enterprise customers to provision and deprovision users from an external identity provider.

**Shared Page**
An internal document published to an external URL with configurable access controls. See [Shared Pages](/guides/shared-pages).

**SSO**
Single Sign-On. Enterprise authentication via SAML or OIDC providers.

## W

**Workspace**
The primary container for content, members, and configuration.
