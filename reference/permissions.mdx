## Roles

Trupeer uses three workspace-level roles:

1. **Admin** — full workspace control including billing and members
2. **Editor** — author and publish content; cannot modify billing or members
3. **Viewer** — read-only access to shared content

Enterprise plans additionally support **SSO-mapped groups** and **SCIM-provisioned users**.

## SSO and SCIM

On Enterprise, role mapping from the identity provider overrides workspace-level role changes. Removing a user in the IdP deprovisions them in Trupeer on the next SCIM sync.
