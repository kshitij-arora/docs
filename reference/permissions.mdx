## Role Hierarchy

Trupeer uses four workspace-level roles, ordered by privilege:

1. **Owner** — terminal authority; can delete the workspace
2. **Admin** — full operational control; cannot modify billing
3. **Member** — content creator; cannot modify workspace settings
4. **Guest** — scoped access to explicitly-shared content only

A user's effective permissions are the union of their workspace role and any page-level grants.

## Organization-Tier Roles

Customers on the Organization tier have two additional roles above workspace Owner:

- **Organization Admin** — can administer any workspace within the organization
- **Organization Owner** — controls organization-wide billing, SSO, and policy

See [Organization](/guides/organization) for configuration details.
