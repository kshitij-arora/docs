## Overview

Every screen in Trupeer is addressable by a stable URL. This map enumerates the canonical route for each surface and its corresponding guide.

## Primary Routes

| Route | Surface | Guide |
|-------|---------|-------|
| `/` | Home — default landing screen | [Home](/guides/home) |
| `/dashboard` | Analytical dashboard | [Dashboard](/guides/dashboard) |
| `/search` | Global search interface | [Search](/guides/search) |
| `/organization` | Organization-level controls | [Organization](/guides/organization) |

## Content Routes

| Route | Surface | Guide |
|-------|---------|-------|

| `/brand-kit` | Brand asset management | [Brand Kit](/guides/brand-kit) |

## Plan Routes

| Route | Surface | Guide |
|-------|---------|-------|

## URL Stability

All routes listed above are considered stable public-facing URLs. Deep-linked query parameters may change between releases; path segments remain backward-compatible.
