## AI Avatar Tab

Add an AI-generated presenter to your videos for a more personal touch.

## Enable AI Avatar

Toggle to show/hide avatar:

- **On** - Avatar appears in video
- **Off** - No avatar overlay

## Select an AI Avatar

Choose from 8+ pre-built avatars:

- Various appearances and styles
- Male and female options
- Different ethnicities

Click **"View all"** to browse the complete library.

## Customization

### Background Color

Set the avatar background:

- Color picker with hex input (e.g., `#FFFFFF`)
- Transparent options
- Match your brand colors

### Avatar Layouts

Click **"Modify avatar layouts"** to adjust:

- **Position** - Where avatar appears
- **Size** - How large the avatar is
- **Animation** - Movement style

## Custom Avatars

<Note>
  Custom avatars are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

### Create Custom Avatar

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Avatars**
2. Click **"+ Create a Custom Avatar"**
3. Upload reference images
4. AI generates your avatar

### Connect External Avatars

Integrate third-party avatar services:

1. Go to Brand Kit → Avatars
2. Click **"Connect your API Key"**
3. Enter your API credentials

## Timeline Integration

Add avatars at specific points:

- Click **"+ Add avatar"** in timeline
- Position avatar appearance
- Set duration

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Consistent Presenter">
    Use the same avatar across videos for brand recognition.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Match Voice">
    Choose an avatar that matches your selected AI voice.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Strategic Placement">
    Add avatars during introduction/conclusion, not throughout.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
