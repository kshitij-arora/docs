## AI Voice Tab

Select from multiple AI voices to narrate your video professionally.

## Options

### Use Original Voice

Toggle to use your original recorded voice instead of AI:

- **On** - Keep your original narration
- **Off** - Replace with AI voice

### Select Voiceover

Choose from available AI voices:

```
Example: "Duggal - Male, Punjabi, Narration, Soft"
```

Voice attributes include:
- **Name** - Voice identifier
- **Gender** - Male/Female
- **Language** - Primary language
- **Style** - Narration, Conversational, etc.
- **Tone** - Soft, Professional, Energetic, etc.

## Custom Voices

<Note>
  Custom voices are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

### Creating Custom Voices

Click **"Manage Custom Voices"** to:

1. Upload voice samples
2. Train AI on your voice
3. Use your custom voice in videos

### External Voice Integration

Connect third-party voice services via API:

1. Go to Brand Kit → Voices
2. Click **"Connect your API Key"**
3. Enter credentials for external service
