## Music Tab

Add background music to create a more engaging video experience.

## Options

### Add Background Music

Toggle to enable/disable background music:

- **On** - Music plays throughout video
- **Off** - No background music

### Select Background Music

Choose from the music library:

```
Available tracks include:
- "Hope" (Default)
- "Upbeat"
- "Corporate"
- "Calm"
- And more...
```

### Modify Music Volume

Adjust the volume slider (0-10):

- **Lower values (1-3)** - Subtle background
- **Medium values (4-6)** - Balanced with voice
- **Higher values (7-10)** - Prominent music

<Tip>
  Keep music volume low (around 3-5) so it doesn't overpower the voiceover.
</Tip>

## Custom Music

<Note>
  Custom music upload is available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

Upload your own tracks:

1. Go to **Brand Kit → Music**
2. Click **"+ Add New Music"**
3. Upload your audio file
4. Use in any video

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Match the Mood">
    Choose music that matches your content - upbeat for demos, calm for tutorials.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep It Low">
    Background music should complement, not compete with narration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="License Check">
    Ensure you have rights to use any custom music you upload.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
