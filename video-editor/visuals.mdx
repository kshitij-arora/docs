## Visuals Tab

The Visuals tab has two sub-sections: **Background** and **Scenes**.

## Background

### Add Background

Toggle to show/hide video background styling:

- **On** - Apply background effects
- **Off** - Show raw screen recording

### Default Backgrounds

Choose from 8+ gradient presets:

- Blue gradients
- Pink/Purple gradients
- Green gradients
- Multi-color options

Click **"View all"** to see the complete library.

### Custom Background

Upload your own background:

1. Click the upload icon in "Custom background"
2. Select an image file
3. Apply to your video

<Note>
  Custom backgrounds are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

### Shadows

Add depth with shadow effects:

| Option | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **None** | No shadow |
| **Light** | Subtle shadow |
| **Medium** | Balanced shadow |
| **Heavy** | Prominent shadow |

### Rounded Corners

Adjust corner radius with the slider:

- **0** - Sharp corners
- **24** (default) - Moderate rounding
- **50+** - Very rounded

### Padding

Control space around your content:

- **0** - No padding
- **16** (default) - Comfortable padding
- **32+** - More breathing room

---

## Scenes

Manage intro and outro scenes.

### Intro Scene

Opening title card with:

- Timestamp indicator (e.g., 0:00 - 0:02)
- Click to customize design
- Add from script section

### Outro Scene

Closing card with:

- Call-to-action options
- Contact information
- Branding elements

<Tip>
  Custom intros and outros are available on **Scale** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Tip>
