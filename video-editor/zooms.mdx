## Zooms Tab

Zoom effects help emphasize important actions and keep viewers engaged.

## Add Zoom Effects

Toggle to enable/disable zoom functionality:

- **On** - Zooms appear in timeline
- **Off** - No zoom effects

## Adding Zooms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Zooms">
    Turn on the "Add zoom effects" toggle
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hover on Timeline">
    Move your cursor over the timeline where you want a zoom
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to Add">
    Click to place a zoom marker
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust">
    Click the marker to edit zoom properties
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Zoom Properties

When editing a zoom, you can adjust:

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Start** | When zoom begins |
| **End** | When zoom ends |
| **Area** | Region to zoom into |
| **Intensity** | How much to zoom |

## Timeline Indicators

Zooms appear as **orange markers** on the timeline:

```
Timeline:
[Intro] ─── 🔍Zoom ──────── 🔍Zoom ─── [Outro]
```

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Zoom on Actions">
    Add zooms when you click buttons, enter text, or perform important actions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Don't Overuse">
    Too many zooms can be distracting. Use sparingly for maximum impact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Time It Right">
    Zoom should start slightly before the action and end shortly after.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  AI automatically suggests zoom points based on your cursor movements - look for auto-added zooms!
</Tip>
